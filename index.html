This web page visualizes the data on population in 2020.

Density world Map

This world map visualizes the countries based on their density variations. A chloropeth map is used for this visualization as the population data is already normalized under the property density which gives a ratio of population to land area of a particular country.

Countries with promising future

The bubble plot visualizes the distribution of urban population , and median age of countries which signifies the developement and youthfulness implying to a promising future.
